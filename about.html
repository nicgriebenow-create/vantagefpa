Who Is Behind It

Built from the company side of the table

Vantage FP&A LLC is a Texas company run by Nicolas Griebenow, based in Austin and working with companies nationwide.

The background is PE-backed company reporting from inside the finance function. Direct stakeholder reporting to a private equity sponsor, live budget cycles under board scrutiny, and multi-entity consolidations for operating companies. The systems and the diligence support are designed around what actually matters in a GP review and in a deal room, learned from inside the seat rather than from the outside of it.

That seat is also where the problem came from. The monthly work of pulling exports, rebuilding the same schedules, and reformatting the same package is real work that a system does better, and the analysis it crowds out is the part a person is actually needed for. Vantage exists to move that line, and to do it in a way the finance team can verify rather than take on faith.

Vantage is early by design and says so plainly. There is no client roster to point at yet. What there is instead is a system you can open, a trace you can follow from a headline figure to the source row that produced it, and an engagement structure that keeps the risk on this side of the table while the first reference engagements get built. A buyer evaluating a new vendor is entitled to ask how they would know the work is right, and the answer to that question is the product.

How We Work

Three things that do not change

These hold on a diligence engagement, on a build, and on the retainer that follows one. They are the operating rules, not preferences.

01
Everything We Produce Is Yours to Keep and Check

Every workbook, model, and deck is delivered formula-driven and self-contained. You can open it, audit every number down to the cell, and keep it, with no software license and no multi-year platform contract underneath it.

02
The Machine Proves, a Person Decides

Deterministic code produces the figures and the commentary. Anything that needs a judgment call is handed over identified and evidenced rather than quietly sized, because a number that requires judgment should carry the name of the person who exercised it.

03
Scope Is Agreed Before the Work Starts

What ships and what does not is written down before anything begins, including the parts that are outside the engagement. Vantage FP&A LLC is not a CPA firm and does not perform audit, review, compilation, or attest engagements, and that is stated at the start rather than discovered at delivery.
